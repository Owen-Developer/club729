My Story
About Me
Club 729

Hi, I’m Finley, I’m 18, and I started Club 729 because I noticed so many young people wanting to make money online but having no real guidance, structure, or people around them to learn from. I wanted to create a space where motivated individuals can not only learn how to make their first income online, but also connect, collaborate, and grow together. Club 729 is about developing real skills, taking action, and staying accountable — through weekly check-ins, 1-on-1 support, and both online and in-person events. My goal is to bring driven people together, share the lessons I’ve learned, and help them create real value and results — without hype or shortcuts.

 My Business

Alongside that, I’m the CEO & Co-Founder of NEXT DESIGN, a web design and software development agency that’s also integrating AI into the software solutions we create. Before NEXT DESIGN, I ran a marketing agency for two years, which gave me firsthand experience helping businesses grow online. At NEXT DESIGN, I handle strategy, sales, and client relationships, focusing on clear communication and real results. We don’t just design websites or build software — we help businesses create online platforms and tools that attract clients, drive growth, and deliver real impact. Long term, I want NEXT DESIGN to become a full digital partner for businesses, supporting every aspect of their online presence and growth.
